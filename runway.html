Runway Show

Join us is this spectacular three-part runway show, featuring Canada’s own, up and coming local fashion brands. The runway show will begin at 6:20 PM and there will be a pop-up shop experience where you can shop these brands at 7:45 PM.

HeartsRFallin

HeartsRFallin; a representation of the past, present & future of the World. The brand focuses on creating fashionable garments based on real life & not fiction. While conceptualizing LIFE in the form of ART; BLOOD, SWEAT & TEARS is what we all have to go through. HeartsRFallin symbolizes the beauty in it all.

Knife Circus

Knife Circus is a brand out of Ottawa that focuses on custom tailoring hand sewn garments. Attention to detail and eye-catching looking is what gives this Knife Circus its danger.
