About

TheTomorrowShow is a show highlighting the bright minds of tomorrow. During this event a number of artists will take the stage to show off their creations. These artists were selected because they are forming the future landscape of our local art. Join us and enjoy a three-part runway show, followed by a three-part live musical act. Come ready to be inspired.

Schedule

TheTomorrowShow will be taking place the night of June 17th 2023 In Ottawa ON.

  1. Introduction - 6:00 PM
  2. RunwayShow - 6:20 PM
  3. Live Music - 7:00 PM
  4. Pop-Up Shop -7:45 PM

Ticket Options

General Admission

General Admission gives access to the runway show, live music, and pop-up shop experience.

V.I.P.

VIP access gives reserved seating for runway show and live music, as well as access to the VIP Lounge.
